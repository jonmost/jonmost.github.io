What is TechFAR Hub?

The TechFAR Hub provides rescources to navigate the world of Digital Service Acquisition

In the TechFAR Hub you will be able to learn everything you need to plan and execute a digital service acquisition. You will also find rescources to engage with the Acquisition Community to ask questions and share your experiences to help others. Finally, the TechFAR Hub provides powerful tools and templates to get you started on your very own Digital Service Acquisition!

  • Discover It

    Find training, events, read case studies, and more!

  • Discuss It

    Share your experiences, read about others, or ask questions!

  • Do It

    Find Tools to get started on your acquisition!
