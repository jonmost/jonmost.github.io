TechFAR Hub

The TechFAR Hub provides rescources to apply the best practices to the world of Digital Service Acquisition across U.S. federal government

In the TechFAR Hub you will be able to learn everything you need to plan and execute a digital service acquisition. You will also find rescources to engage with the Acquisition Community to ask questions and share your experiences to help others. Finally, the TechFAR Hub provides powerful tools and templates to get you started on your very own Digital Service Acquisition!

Let's Get Started!

Implementing a digital service acquisition correctly from the start has been demonstrated to greatly reduce cost and risk and improve end-user satisfaction. Before you embark on your acquisition you should read the Digital Service Playbook and The TechFar Handbook!

Watch a video

Not all training has to be done in the classroom! Specialized videos are a great rescource for discovering new acquisition strategies. The links below provide valueable lessons from acquisition experts that can help you better understand the oppertunities, methods, and challanges around Digital Service Acquisitions. More videos will be available soon, but to get started, visit FAI and checkout Agile Acquisition 101.

Listen to a Podcast

Whether you are driving in your car or sitting at your desk, podcasts are a great way to learn about Digital Service Acquisitions. Anne Rung, Director of OFPP, has created an incredible series of podcasts called "Behind the Buy." Click on one of the audio links below and relax as you listen to audio stories told by members of the Federal acquisition workforce who have successfully executed best practice IT contracting strategies from the TechFAR and Digital Services Playbook to help their agency meet its mission.
